# Digital Vidhyalaya

Digital Vidhyalaya is a Nepali-built operating system for schools and colleges. It connects administrators, teachers, students, and guardians through a single interface so every workflow stays transparent and on schedule.

## What the software handles
- **Attendance automation** – biometric, RFID, or manual roll-calls with instant guardian alerts.
- **Smart notices & announcements** – publish urgent updates, exam schedules, or holiday reminders across web, mobile, and SMS.
- **Academic modules & routines** – manage lesson plans, subject resources, assignments, and classroom routines from one timetable.
- **Fee & finance journeys** – digitize invoices, concessions, settlements, and reminders with secure payment logs.
- **Events and calendars** – plan events, PTA meetings, and activity calendars with RSVP tracking.
- **Secure messaging** – enable monitored teacher-student-guardian conversations for feedback and follow-ups.
- **Leave requests & approvals** – capture student or staff leave, substitutions, and approvals in minutes.
- **Dashboard analytics** – generate compliance-ready reports on attendance, assessments, transport, and finances.

## Why schools adopt it
- Built specifically for Nepali curricula, governance rules, and fee structures.
- Works in both English and Nepali, with offline-friendly SMS bridges for remote communities.
- Delivered with on-ground onboarding experts who digitize records, train staff, and provide ongoing support.

Digital Vidhyalaya keeps admissions, academics, finance, communication, and community engagement in one reliable platform so educators can focus on teaching.
